About Flip Phone Resale

At Flip Phone Resale, we specialize in buying, refurbishing, and reselling quality electronics. From smartphones and laptops to tablets and accessories, our mission is to give devices a second life while saving you money and reducing electronic waste.

Our Story

Founded in Gilbert, Arizona, Flip Phone Resale started as a small, home‑based operation and has grown into a trusted source for affordable, reliable devices. We pride ourselves on transparency, clear documentation, and exceptional customer service.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✔️ Every device is tested, refurbished, and backed by our quality guarantee.
  • ✔️ Clear, legally binding documentation for every transaction.
  • ✔️ Competitive pricing and honest quotes for devices you want to sell.
  • ✔️ Friendly, reliable service you can count on.
