Our 3-Phase Certification Process

Every device, from a laptop to a smartphone, must pass our 3-Phase inspection to be certified for sale.

Phase 1: Physical & Hardware Inspection

[cite_start]Before we even turn it on, we conduct a full physical assessment. [cite: 237]

Phase 2: Comprehensive Benchmark Testing

[cite_start]This is where we push the hardware to its limit using industry-standard software to ensure 100% stable performance. [cite: 47]
