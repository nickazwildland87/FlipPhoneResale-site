Shipping Information

How We Get Your Tech to You

Order Processing Time

All orders are processed and shipped within **one (1) business day** (Monday - Friday) of your purchase. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be shipped on the next business day.

Shipping Methods & Costs

We offer the following shipping options for all domestic orders within the United States:

  • Standard Shipping (5-7 Business Days): $4.99
  • Expedited Shipping (2-3 Business Days): $12.99
  • Free Shipping: All orders over $100 automatically qualify for free Standard Shipping.

Shipment Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email confirmation containing your tracking number. You can use this number to track your package via the carrier's website.

International Shipping

At this time, we only ship to addresses within the United States. We do not offer international shipping.
