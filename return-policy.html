Return Policy

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee on All Purchases

Our 30-Day Guarantee

At Flip Phone Resale, your satisfaction is our top priority. We stand behind every product we sell with a comprehensive 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not 100% satisfied with your purchase for any reason, we'll make it right—no questions asked.

This hassle-free policy applies to all refurbished electronics purchased from our store. Whether it's a minor issue or you simply changed your mind, we're here to help.

Return Eligibility

  • Time Frame: Returns must be initiated within 30 days of the original delivery date.
  • Device Condition: The device must be in the same condition as received, with only normal wear and tear acceptable.
  • Original Accessories: All original items must be returned (charger, cables, box, documentation, etc.).
  • No User Damage: The device must not have new user-inflicted damage such as cracks, water damage, or significant scratches.
  • Account Status: All personal accounts (iCloud, Google, Samsung, etc.) must be signed out and the device factory reset.
  • Proof of Purchase: Your original order confirmation or receipt is required.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Devices returned after 30 days from delivery date
  • Products with water damage or physical damage beyond normal wear
  • Devices with cracked screens or broken components
  • Items missing original accessories or packaging
  • Devices with locked accounts or PIN codes
  • Items purchased from third-party sellers (clearance/sale items may have different policies)

How to Start a Return

The return process is simple and straightforward:

  1. Contact Our Support Team: Email returns@flipphoneresale.com with your order ID and reason for return. Include photos of the device if applicable. You can also call us at (480) 382-6597 (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM MST).
  2. Receive Your RMA Number: We'll respond within 24 hours with your Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and a pre-paid return shipping label.
  3. Prepare Your Device:
    • Factory reset the device or sign out of all accounts
    • Include all original accessories (charger, cables, etc.)
    • Pack securely in original packaging if available
    • Include your RMA number in the package
  4. Ship Your Device: Attach the pre-paid shipping label to the package and drop it off at your nearest carrier location (USPS, UPS, or FedEx). We recommend using tracked shipping.
  5. We Inspect & Process: Once we receive your device (typically 3-5 business days), we inspect it to verify eligibility. If approved, we'll process your full refund to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Refund Details

  • Full Refund Amount: You'll receive a 100% refund of your purchase price, excluding original shipping costs.
  • Refund Timeline: Refunds are processed 5-7 business days after inspection approval.
  • Original Payment Method: Refunds are issued to the same payment method used for purchase (credit card, debit card, PayPal, etc.).
  • Shipping Costs: Return shipping is free (pre-paid label provided). Original shipping charges are non-refundable.
  • Processing Issues: If you don't see the refund within 10 business days, contact your bank or payment provider as they may take additional time to process.

Our Inspection Process

When your return arrives, our quality team will:

  1. Verify the device matches your order
  2. Inspect for damage beyond normal wear and tear
  3. Test all hardware functionality
  4. Confirm all original accessories are included
  5. Verify the device is properly reset with no locked accounts

If we determine the device is not eligible for return due to damage or condition issues, we'll contact you with a detailed explanation and photos before processing any return decisions.

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange your device for a different model or the same device in different condition, we'd be happy to help! Contact us at returns@flipphoneresale.com and we can arrange an exchange within your 30-day window. Exchanges are subject to availability and price adjustments if applicable.
